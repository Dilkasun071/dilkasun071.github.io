Salon Policies

Last updated: February 2026

Our policies are designed to ensure a safe, welcoming, and exceptional experience for every guest. We appreciate your understanding and cooperation.

Booking Policy

We highly recommend booking in advance to secure your preferred time slot, especially for weekends and bridal appointments. Walk-ins are welcome but subject to availability. Online and phone bookings are confirmed via SMS or WhatsApp.

Cancellation Policy

We understand plans change. We request:

  • 24 hours notice for individual service cancellations
  • 48 hours notice for group bookings (3+ clients)
  • 72 hours notice for bridal packages

Repeated late cancellations may result in prepayment being required for future bookings.

Children Policy

Children under 12 are welcome for age-appropriate services (haircuts, nail painting). For safety reasons, children who are not receiving services should not be left unattended in the salon. We appreciate your understanding in ensuring a peaceful environment for all guests.

Colour & Chemical Service Policy

For certain chemical services (permanent colour, relaxers, keratin treatments), a patch test performed 48 hours prior to the appointment is mandatory for new clients or those who have not received such services in the past 12 months. We reserve the right to decline services where a client's hair condition may be at risk of damage.

Satisfaction Guarantee

We are committed to your satisfaction. If you are not completely happy with your service, please let us know before leaving the salon. We will do our best to address your concerns at no additional charge. Redo requests made within 7 days of the service will be considered at management's discretion.

Personal Belongings

New Favourite Salon is not responsible for any lost or stolen personal items. Please keep your valuables secure. We recommend leaving jewellery at home prior to services such as facials or spa treatments.

Privacy Policy

We collect and store your personal information (name, contact details, service history) solely for the purpose of providing our services and communicating appointment reminders. We do not sell or share your data with third parties. You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us.

Photography Policy

We love sharing client transformations! With your permission, we may photograph your results for use on our social media and promotional materials. You will always be asked for consent before any photo is taken or published. You have the right to decline at any time.

Code of Conduct

New Favourite Salon is a respectful, inclusive space. We do not tolerate any form of harassment, discrimination, or aggressive behaviour toward our staff or other clients. We reserve the right to refuse or terminate service for anyone who violates this policy.
